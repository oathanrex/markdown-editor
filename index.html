Why Use This Online Markdown Editor?

Let's face it: writing documentation or blog posts shouldn't be a hassle. You shouldn't have to sign up, pay a subscription, or install heavy software just to format some text. That's why we built this **free, privacy-first editor**.

Whether you're a developer tweaking a README, a student writing a paper with math formulas, or just someone who loves the simplicity of Markdown, this tool is designed to get out of your way and let you write.

Designed for Real World Use Cases

  • For Developers: Write GitHub-ready documentation with full GFM support and syntax highlighting for 50+ languages.
  • For Students & Researchers: seamless LaTeX $E=mc^2$ support makes technical writing a breeze.
  • For Privacy Advocates: Your keystrokes never leave your device. Everything is saved locally in your browser.
  • For Offline Work: On a flight or spotty Wi-Fi? The app works 100% offline once loaded.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account or pay?

No and No. This tool is completely free and open-source. There are no logins, no paywalls, and no hidden "pro" features. Just open the page and write.

Is my writing safe? Where is it stored?

Your data never leaves your computer. Unlike other cloud editors, we rely entirely on your browser's Local Storage. We cannot read, see, or sell your documents because we simply don't have access to them.

Can I use this without an internet connection?

Yes! This is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once you visit the site, it installs necessary files to your device, allowing you to open and use the editor fully offline, just like a native desktop app.

How do I save my work locally?

While autosave keeps your work safe in the browser, you can click the Export button to download your file as a standardized `.md` Markdown file, a raw `.html` file, or generate a PDF for sharing.
